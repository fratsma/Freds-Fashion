ABOUT THE BRAND

Yves Saint Laurent created the fashion brand under his own name in 1961 in Paris. Like many of the other luxury fashion houses, YSL started out creating womenswear as it has grown, it has developed a well-respected menswear line. When Saint Laurent died in 2008, YSL had a rocky few years and some of their boutiques had to close. However, once Heid Slimane replaced Stefano Pilati as creative director, YSL was reborn and was gaining popularity rapidly. Fast forward to today and Saint Laurent is one of the largest and beloved fashion houses in the luxury fashion industry.
