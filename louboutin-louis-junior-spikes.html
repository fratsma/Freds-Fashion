ABOUT THE BRAND

Christian Louboutin created the iconic red soled luxury shoe brand in 1991 in Paris. He is most well-known for bringing stiletto heels back in fashion and his goal was to make a woman look sexy, beautiful, to make her legs look as long as [he] can . Louboutin has also made a name for themselves in the men’s shoe industry and have incorporated the red soles into their designs. They also have a large accessories industry which include phone cases and wallets.
