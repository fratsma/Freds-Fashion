ABOUT THE BRAND

Moncler was founded in 1952 by Monestier-de-Clermont in France. The brand is most famous for their wide collection of coats and jackets, but they also have an extensive menswear and womenswear collection. Their polo shirts are very popular as are their jumpers and knitwear. Moncler rose to popularity when they became the official supplier for the French Ski team in 1968 and have gain customer loyalty since then.
