ABOUT THE BRAND

Valentino was created by Valentino Garavani who created the famous fashion brand under his own name in 1960. As many of the fashion houses in the luxury fashion industry, Valentino is most famous for their women’s clothing but with that being said, they have a very popular menswear collection. One of Valentino’s most famous categories of fashion is wedding dresses and they have designed wedding dresses for celebrities such as Anne Hathaway, Jennifer Lopez and Elizabeth Taylor.
