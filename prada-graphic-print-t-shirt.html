ABOUT THE BRAND

Prada was created by founder Mario Prada in 1913 in Milan. There are over 620 boutiques worldwide and they have made their name due to their unique designs and originality. Since the 1990’s Prada has become a brand known for their luxury status and premium quality. The brand has had many features in films such as Devil Wears Prada and 10 Things I hate about you. Prada has impeccable craftsmanship and only uses the highest quality of materials so you always know you are getting an item that will last you a lifetime.
