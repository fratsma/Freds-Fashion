WANT TO WRITE FOR US?

Do you want to be a guest blogger for Fred's Fashion? If you think you have what it takes, drop us an email at fredsfashionboutique@gmail.com. The blog post can be any length (ideally no less than 500 words) and can be about any topic that is relevent in men's fashion.

In return, you will recieve: a link to your website, a feature in our monthly email newsletter and if it generates enough pageviews, a share of the ad revenue. It will also be shared on our social media platforms, such as Twitter, Facebook and Reddit.

Fred's Fashion is still a new website, and we are trying to increase the amount of content that we provide to our readers. We think that having guest contributers will benefit both us and you, becuase it will give us more content to publish, and will generate more traffic to your website / blog due to the backlink. We will only publish high quality blog posts becuase we believe in quality over quantity, so before submitting a blog post, please make sure that it is of high quality.

If you would like any more information, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to reading your blog posts :)
