ABOUT THE BRAND

Canada Goose was created by Sam Tick in 1957 in Canada. They are most known for their extremely high quality, warm and durable winter coats and parkas. Their coats have been worn on expeditions to Antarctica and up Mount Everest. Their coats have also been featured in multiple Hollywood films such as The Day After Tomorrow and have also been worn by countless celebrities.
