ABOUT THE BRAND

Dior was created by Christian Dior in 1946 and has grown to be one of the largest and well know luxury fashion designers. It has been the home to iconic bags such as the Dior Saddle Bag and their theatrical gowns. Even though Dior is most famous for women’s clothing, it has an extensive menswear collection that has grown in popularity over the years. Built with high quality craftsmanship and materials, Dior will always be one of the favorites here at Fred’s Fashion.
