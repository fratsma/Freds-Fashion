ABOUT THE BRAND

Fendi was created in 1925 by Adele and Edoardo Fendi and it started as a fur and leather shop in Rome. The fashion house was a family run business with the 5 daughters of Adele and Edoardo taking over the business. Since then it has gained an immense amount popularity with celebrities and the public with its creation of the baguette bag and their iconic FF logo that was created by one of the best creative designers, Karl Lagerfeld. The FF logo stands Fun Fur, which is a controversial meaning but that hasn’t stopped Fendi from becoming the fashion giant it is today.
