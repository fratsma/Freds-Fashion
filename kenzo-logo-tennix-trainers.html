ABOUT THE BRAND

Kenzo Takada founded Kenzo in 1970 in Paris. The brand made their name creating handmade women’s clothing, with the focus on high quality and adventurous designs. 15 years later, Kenzo released their menswear collection which created a lot of popularity for the brand. Kenzo’s signature Tiger graphic was released in their 2012 fall collection and even though the design team didn’t think that it was a good design, it became extremely popular and their stock ran out within hours of their release.
