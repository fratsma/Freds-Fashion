ABOUT THE BRAND

Versace was created in 1978 by Gianni Versace in Milan. It is definitely one of the most well-known brands in the fashion industry and has focused on Greek mythology with their logo being the Medusa head. The reason why Gianni Versace chose the Medusa head was because in Greek mythology she made people fall in love with her and once they fell in love they could do nothing to stop it, and Versace was hoping that people would feel the same way with his products. Now that Gianni Versace is dead, the creative designer is Donatella Versace (sister of Gianni) but the brand is now owned by Michael Kors.
