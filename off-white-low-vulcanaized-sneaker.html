ABOUT THE BRAND

Off-White was created in 2012 by Virgil Abloh in Milan and since then the brand has become more and more popular and has had a lot of celebrity interest. They have also had a lot of collaborations with other luxury fashion brands such as Nike, Moncler, Louis Vuitton and even Ikea! Off-White have released countless successful designs and celebrities all over the world have been seen wearing them. One of Off-White’s most well know designs is their yellow industrial belt that has been seen being worn around the globe. Off-White may be a relatively new brand but that doesn’t mean they should be underestimated.
